# Pricing Policy

**Document ID:** PRICING-001

**Version:** 2.1

**Last Updated:** June 2026

---

# Overview

This document defines SenAI's pricing model, discount eligibility, billing rules, and enterprise engagement process. Customer Support, Sales, and AI agents must reference this document when answering pricing or billing questions.

---

# Subscription Plans

## Starter

* Monthly Price: $29/user/month
* Maximum Users: 10
* Email Support
* Basic Analytics
* Community Access

---

## Standard

* Monthly Price: $79/user/month
* Unlimited Users
* Priority Email Support
* Workflow Automation
* API Access
* Standard Analytics

---

## Enterprise

* Custom Pricing
* Dedicated Success Manager
* 24/7 Support
* Custom Integrations
* SSO & SAML
* Advanced Security
* Unlimited API Usage

Enterprise pricing is determined through the Sales team and cannot be generated automatically.

---

# Non-Profit Discount

**Policy ID: PRICING-NPO-001**

Eligible registered non-profit organizations receive:

* 30% discount on Standard Plan
* Free migration assistance
* Annual billing only

Required verification:

* Government registration
* Non-profit certificate
* Organization email domain

The AI agent may mention the availability of this discount but must not approve eligibility automatically.

---

# Educational Discount

Eligible educational institutions receive:

* 20% Standard Plan discount
* Student programs available upon request

Verification is mandatory.

---

# Pro-Rata Billing

**Policy ID: BILLING-PRORATA-001**

Customers upgrading during an active billing cycle are charged only for the remaining portion of the cycle.

Formula:

New Plan Cost
−
Unused Previous Plan Credit
===========================

Pro-Rata Charge

Downgrades become effective at the beginning of the next billing cycle.

---

# Upgrade Policy

Upgrades:

* Effective immediately
* Features unlocked instantly
* Pro-rata billing applies

Downgrades:

* Effective next billing cycle
* Existing features remain available until renewal

---

# Enterprise Opportunities

Potential enterprise customers should be routed to Sales if any of the following apply:

* More than 100 users
* Compliance requirements
* Custom contract requests
* Procurement process
* RFP submissions
* Annual contract negotiations

The AI agent should never generate enterprise pricing estimates.

---

# Pricing Objections

When customers express pricing concerns:

1. Identify customer type
2. Check eligibility for discounts
3. Recommend appropriate plan
4. Mention annual billing savings when applicable
5. Escalate enterprise negotiations to Sales

---

# AI Agent Guidance

The AI agent may:

* Explain pricing tiers
* Explain pro-rata billing
* Mention available discounts
* Recommend plans

The AI agent must NOT:

* Invent discounts
* Generate enterprise quotes
* Negotiate pricing
* Override published policies
