# Refund & Customer Retention Policy

**Document ID:** REFUND-001

**Version:** 3.2

**Last Updated:** June 2026

---

# Overview

This policy defines SenAI's refund eligibility, customer retention strategy, exception handling process, and AI agent response guidelines.

Customer satisfaction is a priority, but refunds must remain consistent with company policy and legal obligations.

---

# Standard Refund Policy

Customers are eligible for a full refund if ALL of the following conditions are met:

* Request submitted within 14 calendar days of purchase
* Account is in good standing
* No fraud indicators detected
* No enterprise custom agreement overrides exist

---

# Refund Window

**Policy ID: REFUND-002**

Refund requests received after 14 calendar days are normally declined.

Instead, Customer Success may offer:

* Account credits
* Service extensions
* Plan downgrades
* Retention discounts

The AI agent must never promise a refund outside the policy window.

---

# Enterprise Customers

Enterprise contracts are governed by signed agreements.

Refunds require approval from:

* Account Executive
* Finance
* Customer Success Director

The AI agent must escalate enterprise refund requests.

---

# Churn Retention Playbook

**Policy ID: RETENTION-001**

When a customer expresses dissatisfaction, the following escalation ladder should be followed:

Level 1

* Apologize
* Acknowledge concerns
* Provide clarification

Level 2

* Offer account credits
* Offer onboarding assistance
* Offer success manager consultation

Level 3

* Escalate to Customer Success Manager

Level 4

* Executive review

---

# Public Review Threats

If a customer mentions:

* Trustpilot
* G2
* Capterra
* Reddit
* Twitter/X
* LinkedIn
* Public review

the AI agent must:

1. Mark Priority = High
2. Escalate to Customer Success
3. Recommend retention workflow
4. Trigger Reputation Intelligence lookup

The AI agent must not argue with the customer.

---

# AI Chatbot Misinformation

**Policy ID: AI-001**

If a customer states that an AI assistant previously provided incorrect information:

The AI agent must:

* acknowledge the concern
* retrieve the latest refund policy
* avoid repeating incorrect information
* escalate for human review if policy conflict exists

The AI agent must NOT:

* blame previous employees
* admit legal liability
* invent new refund rules

---

# Exceptions

Exceptions may be granted for:

* confirmed billing errors
* duplicate charges
* platform outages causing data loss
* executive approval

All exceptions must be logged for audit purposes.

---

# Agent Guidance

The AI agent MAY:

* explain refund eligibility
* explain policy timelines
* offer account credits
* recommend retention options

The AI agent MUST NOT:

* guarantee refunds
* negotiate custom compensation
* override policy
* approve enterprise exceptions
