# Overview

This document provides official guidance regarding SenAI's compliance programs, data privacy commitments, security certifications, and regulatory processes.

AI agents must reference this document when answering compliance or legal questions.

---

# HIPAA

## Business Associate Agreement (BAA)

Policy ID: HIPAA-001

SenAI offers a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) for Enterprise customers processing Protected Health Information (PHI).

Requirements:

* Enterprise subscription
* Signed agreement
* Security review
* Compliance approval

The AI agent may confirm BAA availability but must not generate or approve legal agreements.

---

# GDPR

## Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

Policy ID: GDPR-001

All Enterprise customers may request a Data Processing Agreement.

Requests are processed by the Legal & Compliance team.

Expected processing time:

3–5 business days.

---

## Article 20 Data Portability

Policy ID: GDPR-ARTICLE20-001

Customers may request an export of their personal data.

Process:

1. Identity verification
2. Compliance review
3. Data export generation
4. Secure delivery

Target completion:

Within 30 calendar days.

The AI agent must acknowledge the request and immediately escalate to Compliance.

Automatic fulfillment is prohibited.

---

## Right to Erasure

Policy ID: GDPR-DELETE-001

Deletion requests require:

* Identity verification
* Outstanding invoice review
* Legal retention check

Deletion may be delayed where legally required.

---

# SOC 2

Policy ID: SOC2-001

SenAI maintains:

SOC 2 Type II certification.

Annual audits are performed by independent third-party auditors.

Detailed audit reports are available under NDA.

---

# Data Residency

Supported regions:

* United States
* European Union
* India

Enterprise customers may request region-specific deployments.

Migration requests require Customer Success approval.

---

# Security Controls

Implemented controls include:

* Encryption at rest
* TLS 1.3 in transit
* Multi-factor authentication
* Role-based access control
* Continuous monitoring
* Audit logging

---

# Compliance Escalation Rules

The AI agent must immediately escalate if an email contains:

* GDPR
* HIPAA
* Privacy complaint
* Data breach
* Legal notice
* Regulatory inquiry

These requests must never receive an autonomous final response.

---

# AI Agent Guidance

The AI agent MAY:

* explain published compliance programs
* acknowledge compliance requests
* explain expected timelines

The AI agent MUST NOT:

* provide legal advice
* interpret regulations
* promise compliance outcomes
* generate contracts
